Understanding the Different Types of Roses

People use various methods to classify the different types of roses. You can call upon plant growth forms to break roses down into climbers, ramblers, or shrubs. And you can consider the plant’s origins to distinguish these plants as wild roses, old garden roses, or new garden roses. Another way to classify roses is by their species or cultivar. Many modern roses are not individual species but rather hybrid varieties known by their cultivar names. Fun names like ‘Princess Anne’ and ‘Rio Samba’ may not roll off the tongue, but their prolific flowers and alluring fragrances make them great additions to the garden.
